Four Corners Wellness Agenda

was created by 7 Indigenous youth. We came together to create a small business that would positively affect our communities by sharing parts of Indigenous culture.

The phrase "Four Corners" refers the Medicine wheel, which  embodies the Four Directions, as well as Father Sky, Mother Earth, and Spirit Tree—all of which symbolize dimensions of health and the cycles of life: birth, youth, adult. Each of the Four Directions is typically represented by a distinctive color, such as black, red, yellow, and white.

We collaborate with a variety of different cultures to create a product that highlights Indigenous heritage by incorporating it into your daily lives.

So we hope you enjoy our 16 month Agenda that incorporates Indigenous beliefs based on the Medicine Wheel, teachings from Elders, mental health tips, coloring pages , recipes, key historical dates and much more!
