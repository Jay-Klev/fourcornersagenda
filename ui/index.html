Four Corners Wellness Agenda

We know that life can get stressful and busy, your agenda has been designed with that in mind!

Four Corners Agenda was started by 7 Indigenous youth. We are a small business, currently collaborating with each other's unique cultures. We have created a 16 month agenda book (day planner) that is incorporated with wellness tips and Indigenous beliefs based on the Medicine Wheel,

We collaborated with local BC Indigenous community members such as (Elders, Artists and Business owners) to create something unique that voices the creativity of Indigenous youth.
