What is the Four Corners Agenda?

Our team has come together to create this agenda for "The BC 3C Challenge", an entrepreneurial business challenge for Indigenous youth. We were tasked at creating our own small business within 45 days, in which we could contribute a portion of earnings towards our own communities and charities. Our goal was to create a 16 month agenda that can be fun, inspire indigenous beliefs and promote wellness by passing on the teaching of the medicine wheel. We collaborated with local BC Indigenous community members such as (Elders, Artists and Business owners) to create something unique that voices the creativity of Indigenous youth.
